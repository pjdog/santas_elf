# Santa's Elf Features

## Recipe Finder

The Recipe Finder will help you plan your holiday meals with ease.

### Key Features:

*   **Scaling by Number of Guests:**
    *   Input the number of guests, and the recipe will automatically adjust ingredient quantities.
    *   The UI will clearly show the original recipe yield and the adjusted yield.
*   **Guest Profiles & Preferences:**
    *   Create profiles for guests with dietary restrictions (e.g., vegetarian, gluten-free, allergies).
    *   The elf will filter and suggest recipes that cater to all your guests' needs.
    *   Specify guest preferences (e.g., "kids," "adventurous eaters") for tailored suggestions.
*   **Recipe Discovery:**
    *   Search for recipes by name, ingredients, or occasion (e.g., "Christmas dinner," "Hanukkah dessert").
    *   Browse curated collections of holiday recipes.
    *   Get suggestions based on your past searches and saved recipes.

## Gift Guide

The Gift Guide will help you find the perfect presents for your loved ones.

### Key Features:

*   **Personalized Recommendations:**
    *   Describe the recipient:
        *   Age and gender
        *   Interests and hobbies (e.g., "gardening," "video games," "reading")
        *   Relationship to you (e.g., "mom," "co-worker," "best friend")
    *   The elf will generate a list of gift ideas based on your description.
*   **Budget Management:**
    *   Set a budget for each gift (e.g., "$25," "$50-100").
    *   The elf will filter recommendations to fit your budget.
*   **Gift Tracking:**
    *   Keep track of your gift list, marking items as "to buy," "purchased," and "wrapped."
    *   Avoid duplicate gifts and stay organized.
*   **Shopping Assistance:**
    *   Get links to online stores where you can purchase the recommended gifts.
    *   Compare prices from different retailers.
